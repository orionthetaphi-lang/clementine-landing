Why people come

A soft adventure for families, date days, and anyone needing a slower afternoon.

Clementine blends a premium farm-stay visual style with the approachable joy of meeting lovable Highland cows face to face. Think heirloom blankets, cedar fences, tall grasses, and one very boopable herd.
